About Stage Fright

Stage Fright is a high-energy death metal band known for delivering brutal, electrifying performances that fuse the raw intensity of death metal with modern, aggressive vibes. Formed in 2021 by four friends with a shared love for heavy music, the band quickly evolved from casual jam sessions into a full-scale musical journey. Their crushing sound and ferocious live shows have earned them a dedicated cult like following, positioning Stage Fright as a rising force in the death metal scene. With punishing guitar riffs, guttural vocals, thunderous drums, and bone-rattling basslines, their music is an unrelenting assault on the senses. Stage Fright thrives on high-octane energy, whether they’re tearing up small venues or dominating larger stages, leaving fans in awe long after the final note. Known for their precise musicianship and chaotic stage presence, the band engages with their audience on a visceral level, ensuring that every performance is as intense as it is memorable. In the studio, Stage Fright has been relentlessly crafting original tracks that are set to push the boundaries of death metal. Their music embodies the primal spirit of the genre while incorporating fresh, experimental elements that appeal to both die-hard metalheads and new fans alike. With blistering talent, unapologetic aggression, and a hunger to carve their name into the metal world, Stage Fright isn’t just a band to follow—they’re a force to be reckoned with. Get ready for their upcoming album and live carnage, because Stage Fright is only getting heavier.
